**ResumeIQ** is an advanced AI-powered resume analyzer that evaluates your CV line by line, provides personalized improvements, ATS-friendly suggestions, and creates optimized cover letters instantly.

🌐 **Visit Website:** [https://resumeiq.whitecoder.online/](https://resumeiq.whitecoder.online/)

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🤖 **AI-Powered Analysis:** Upload your resume and let the AI scan it to find areas for improvement and formatting issues.
* 📈 **ATS-Friendly Suggestions:** Get actionable tips to optimize your resume for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to increase your hiring chances.
* ✍️ **Cover Letter Generation:** Automatically generate tailored, professional cover letters based on your resume and target job role.
* 📝 **Line-by-Line Evaluation:** Receive detailed, personalized feedback on your experience, skills, and summary sections.
* ⚡ **Fast & Accurate:** Get real-time analysis and instant results to perfect your application in minutes.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. **Upload Resume:** Go to the website and upload your current CV/Resume.
2. **AI Scan:** ResumeIQ's intelligent AI evaluates the document for ATS compatibility, keywords, and overall structure.
3. **Review Feedback:** Read the personalized suggestions and line-by-line improvements provided by the tool.
4. **Generate Cover Letter:** Use the built-in AI tool to instantly generate a matching cover letter for your next job application.
5. **Apply with Confidence:** Update your resume with the provided suggestions and stand out to recruiters!
